RealityCheck Insights is a companion app that tracks meals, habits, and body data to deliver blunt weekly forecasts. It helps you understand the long-term impact of todayâ€™s decisions.

RealityCheck captures what I eat, how I move, and how I feel. From these inputs, trend lines surface hidden patterns and deliver a weekly recommendation to stay the course or adjust. Users can dive into macro projections or simply follow the next best step the app suggests.

Long-term motivation comes from transparency: RealityCheck does not sugarcoat the outlook. If current habits point to weight gain or energy dips, charts and copy make it clear and deliver concrete countersteps.
