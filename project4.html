RealityCheck is a personal, data-driven self-reflection system that tracks daily habits, routines, and decisions to provide an honest analysis of long-term progress. It focuses on radical honesty, measurable behavior, and clear consequences rather than motivation without data.

What RealityCheck Does

RealityCheck combines daily tracking, automated evaluation, and periodic reflection to make progress or stagnation visible. It captures:

  • Daily entries (discipline, training, nutrition, focus, mood).
  • Body metrics such as weight.
  • Routines and habits.
  • Self-defined goals.

Core Feature: The Reality Check

  • Analyzes historical entries and detects trends.
  • Flags improvement, stagnation, or regression.
  • Delivers direct, honest feedback with no empty motivation.
  • Every Sunday at the same time, a Reality Check arrives with the week’s data and a projection of where you will end up if you continue on the same path.

Technical Setup (High Level)

  • Mobile app: Android (Kotlin / Jetpack Compose).
  • Backend & storage: Firebase.
  • Evaluation logic for trends and projections.
  • Planned extension: AI-supported analysis and forecasting.

Personal Approach

  • Radical honesty: no excuses, no filters.
  • Measurability: only what is measured counts.
  • Consequences: every decision has long-term impact.
