FWTHORN brand preview (private)

FWTHORN is the next project where I built a personal brand across social media and a website. Through this account I document daily life and show what I do to live well, based on how I currently see it and the studies I follow.

I record my workouts, the projects I work on, how I shop, and the routines I keep. The content also promotes RealityCheck and supports a healthy, goal-focused lifestyle. Many men my age feel lost and procrastinate at home or in a job until they realize at 30 or 40 what they could have done in their early twenties. FWTHORN is meant to be a practical, motivating counterexample.

  • Team: Solo project
  • Channels: social media presence and a dedicated website
  • Content: workouts, projects, shopping, and daily routines
  • Purpose: healthy lifestyle guidance and RealityCheck awareness
