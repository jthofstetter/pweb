Civil service at the Red Cross

My civil service at the Red Cross combines technical affinity with direct help for people. In emergency medical services, I support response teams, keep equipment in shape, and learn to stay focused under pressure.

Areas of responsibility:

  • Assisting in emergency medical services and accompanying emergency responses.
  • Maintaining and documenting response vehicles and equipment.
  • Up to 400 km of driving in a single day while transporting patients. Primary area: Waidhofen an der Thaya, with assignments also taking me to Vienna, St. Pölten, and across the edges of Lower Austria.

I successfully completed the emergency medical technician exam and am planning to continue as a volunteer at the Red Cross.
