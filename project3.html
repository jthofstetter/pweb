CoRide is a shared mobility platform that gives passengers transparent insight into available drivers, seat availability, and pricing before booking. The app was built with .NET MAUI and delivers a single codebase for Android and iOS while maintaining native performance.

Drivers publish rides including route, seat count, and vehicle information. Passengers search for their destination, verify the vehicle identity, and confirm the ride directly in the app. Behind the scenes, we orchestrate database flows, authentication, and UI polish for a reliable experience.

  • Team: 3 Builder
  • Technologies: .NET MAUI, C#, SQLite, Azure App Service, MVVM
  • Features: seat-based pricing, driver profiles with vehicle data, dual-platform deployment
  • Future ideas: real-time tracking via SignalR, bilateral ratings, optimized route batching
