IT Systems Engineering, HTL Krems (Campus Zwettl)

The Higher Technical College (HTL) Krems trains students in a five-year, full-time program that blends theory and hands-on labs. The IT Systems Engineering track spans roughly 36–40 contact hours per week and emphasizes practical networking, operating systems, and software engineering projects.

5-year engineering diploma ~36–40 hrs/week in class & labs Cisco, Windows, Linux, virtualization Capstone & industry projects

Curriculum highlights

Networking & infrastructure

Routing, switching, VLAN design, firewalling, and network hardening backed by Cisco lab time every semester.

Systems & deployment

Windows and Linux administration, virtualization, container basics, and automated deployment with a focus on reliability.

Software engineering

C#, Python, database design, and collaborative projects covering requirements, version control, testing, and delivery.

Security & project work

Fundamentals of IT security, risk assessment, and structured project management with customer-style milestones.

What fascinated me

What fascinated me the most were the subjects Artificial Intelligence and ITP (project work). AI resonated with my interests and the idea of building something that thinks independently. ITP appealed to my creativity because we could shape ideas into working prototypes.

Artificial Intelligence

Für mich war KI der spannendste Teil, weil der Gedanke fasziniert, etwas zu entwickeln, das eigenständig denkt, lernt und kluge Entscheidungen trifft.
