IT Systems Engineering, HTL Krems (Campus Zwettl)

The Higher Technical College (HTL) Krems trains students in a five-year, full-time program that blends theory and hands-on labs. The IT Systems Engineering track spans roughly 36–40 contact hours per week and emphasizes practical networking, operating systems, and software engineering projects.

Schwerpunkte kompakt

  • Netzwerkplanung, Routing, Switching, Firewalling.
  • Windows- & Linux-Administration, Virtualisierung, Deployment.
  • Softwareentwicklung (C#, Python), Datenbanken, Versionskontrolle.
  • IT-Security-Grundlagen und Projektmanagement.
